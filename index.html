Our Mission

Technology that serves people, not the other way around

We started this because we were tired of watching useful technology stop at borders, stop at income brackets, stop short of the people who need it most. Africa isn't behind — it's been underserved. We're here to fix that, one product at a time.

“We don’t build for money. We build to improve lives and help people tap into their calling.” — NÉAM & CO, Founding Principle
